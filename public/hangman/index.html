Hangman!














Not gonna lie, this game is a little hard to understand at first glance, but once you know how to play, it will be more intuitive.
You have one moderator who comes up with a word or phrase (spaces are okay) and inputs it in without the guessers looking.
You then give the computer the number of guessers and every guesser's name.
The guessers have to guess this word by typing in any letter on the box at the left or any phrase at the box on the right.
If they guess a correct letter, it will pop up for all of the letters in the word.
If they guess the whole phrase correctly, it will automatically fill in and you win!
There are eleven stages to the hangman. If you guess anything wrong, the hangman will gain a body part.



It is highly recommended to play this game on a computer with a screen at least 13 inches long.
